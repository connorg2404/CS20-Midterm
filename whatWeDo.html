Our Mission

Medford Animal Shelter is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need. We believe every animal deserves a loving home and a second chance at life. Our mission is to provide a safe haven for abandoned, injured, or lost pets and to work with our community to promote responsible pet ownership.

Our Services

  • Animal Rescue: We rescue animals from abusive, neglectful, and hazardous situations and provide them with medical care and emotional support.
  • Adoption Services: We work diligently to find permanent, loving homes for animals. Each pet is matched with a family that suits their needs and personality.
  • Spay/Neuter Program: To combat overpopulation, we provide affordable spay/neuter services to pet owners in our community.
  • Education and Outreach: We host events, workshops, and school programs to educate the public about animal welfare and responsible pet ownership.
  • Foster Care Program: Some animals need extra care before they are ready for adoption. Our foster program allows volunteers to care for these animals in their homes.
  • Veterinary Services: Our on-site veterinary team provides essential medical services to shelter animals, including vaccinations, surgeries, and routine check-ups.

Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteers are the heart of Medford Animal Shelter. We are always looking for passionate individuals to help with various tasks. Here’s how you can get involved:

  • Animal Care: Help feed, groom, and socialize with the animals. Volunteers in this area provide much-needed attention and comfort to animals waiting for their forever homes.
  • Event Planning: Assist with planning and hosting community events, adoption fairs, and fundraisers.
  • Outreach and Education: Help us educate the public on topics like animal welfare and responsible pet ownership by joining our outreach efforts in schools and community centers.
  • Foster Program: Provide temporary homes for animals in need of special care or socialization. This can be a rewarding experience for individuals and families who want to help directly from their homes.
  • Fundraising: Support our shelter by organizing fundraisers and campaigns to generate much-needed funds for our programs.

If you’re interested in volunteering, please contact us through our Contact page for more information!

How Your Donations Help

Your donations enable us to provide essential care and services to animals in need. Here’s how your support makes a difference:

  • Food and Supplies: Donations help us provide high-quality food, bedding, toys, and other necessities for the animals.
  • Medical Treatment: Many animals come to us in need of urgent medical attention. Your donations allow us to provide surgeries, vaccinations, and other life-saving treatments.
  • Facility Maintenance: Keeping the shelter clean, safe, and comfortable is essential. Donations support our ongoing maintenance and improvements.
  • Outreach Programs: Donations fund our community education initiatives and help us promote responsible pet ownership.

Every contribution, big or small, goes directly to supporting our shelter and improving the lives of animals in our care.
